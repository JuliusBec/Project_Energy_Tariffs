Welcome to Energy Tariffs Documentation
========================================

Energy Tariffs is a comprehensive platform for analyzing and comparing energy pricing strategies,
including dynamic tariff calculations, risk analysis, and usage forecasting.

Features
--------

* **Dynamic Energy Pricing**: Calculate and compare different energy tariff models
* **Risk Analysis**: Analyze the financial risks associated with different tariff structures
* **Usage Forecasting**: Predict future energy consumption patterns
* **Price Forecasting**: Forecast energy market prices
* **Household Analysis**: Analyze energy usage patterns for different household types
